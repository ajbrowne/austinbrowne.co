About Me

My name is Austin Browne and I am a third year Computer Science major at Michigan Tech. I love programming, especially when it comes to web and Android development. I have been working in Android for about a year now for organizations such as Target and Mind Trekkers. I also just recently discovered how much fun web dev is, and have since worked on this website and a few side projects such as MTG Search and lovrhatr. I am always thinking and am somewhat of a perfectionist. I have maintained a 3.71 GPA at Michigan Tech while making the Dean's List every semester. I am also a member of the Upsilon Pi Epsilon computer science honor society. So go ahead and browse through some of my projects to see what I've been up to lately!
