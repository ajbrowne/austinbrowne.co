I Play in Sandboxes

Allan is a front-end developer and designer hailing from Los Angeles, California. He thrives on creating interactive and intuitive digital experiences. As he sharpens his skills in coding and design, the results are an ever growing showcase of how far he has come.

His passion and talent are seen in the work he has done for clients including and not limited to FOX Television, Jack in the Box, Disney/ABC Television Group, and Colgate. Through these experiences, Allan has grown into a versatile designer and a developer.

When he’s not in the zone coding, you can find him geeking out over comic books, science, basketball, Racer 5 IPAs, grubbing on spaghetti & meatballs and most importantly, spending time quality time with family and friends. Some call him Batman; if you knew him, you would understand. Overall he’s a pretty cool guy. Sit back and feel free to take a look at his latest work.

payvia

Main Menu

www.usepayvia.com

The Mogreet team was acquired by payvia and we merged as one company. Mogreet combined its powerful mobile messaging platform with payvia’s mobile messaging aggregation service.

A few months after the companies merge I was set to lead the rebranding and redesign of the payvia website. Through a rigorous research and a very tight deadline (20days) I was able to deploy into production a very powerful and engaging website powered by Wordpress. In addition to the website, I also released a new payvia logo and color scheme. I worked 18 days out of the 20 days that I was given to build this site. Overall, this website is one of the best websites I’ve ever outputted.

Responsibilities:
  • Development
  • UX design
  • Graphic design
  • Responsive design
  • Branding

Mogreet

Main Menu

www.mogreet.com

Mogreet has a special place in my heart and I am very proud to be apart of their team. Through Mogreet’s startup culture I was able to cultivate my skillset and make it grow. At a startup you’re required to be able to adapt to different situations and problems. Through Mogreet’s clients and internal projects I expanded my skills of design and development. My naturally willingness to learn in and outside of the workplace synced perfectly with the Mogreet culture.

I designed, built, and maintained Mogreet sites’ such as Express, Enterprise, Developer, and moShare. In addition to Mogreet’s full websites, I also created some fun landing pages. My favorite landing page is our White Paper Landing Page because they let me be as creative and free as I wanted to.
